Who We Are

More Than a Liquor Store — A Community Staple

Express Liquor & Wine was founded with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest spirits, wines, and beers to the people of West Columbia, South Carolina — at prices that make sense and with service that feels personal.

Nestled on Augusta Road, we've spent years building a store that locals trust and visitors discover with delight. Whether you're picking up a case of Corona for the weekend, searching for a rare Scotch single malt, or exploring wines for a dinner party — our doors are open and our team is ready to help.

We believe every bottle tells a story. From the soil of a French vineyard to the copper pot stills of Kentucky, each product on our shelves represents a craft, a culture, and a tradition. We are proud to share that with you.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to West Columbia's go-to spirits destination — here's how Express Liquor & Wine grew over the years.

Est. 2009

Doors Open on Augusta Rd

Express Liquor & Wine opened its doors with a core selection of spirits and a commitment to serving the West Columbia community.

2012

Wine Wall Expansion

We expanded our wine collection significantly, adding hundreds of domestic and international labels to satisfy growing demand.

2016

Premium Spirits & Craft Collection

We launched our premium spirits section and began stocking rare, limited-edition releases and craft distillery products.

2020

Renovation & Brand Refresh

Full store renovation with improved layout, better displays, and expanded product categories including craft beer and specialty liqueurs.

Today

2,000+ Products & Growing

Today we carry over 2,000 products from 50+ countries and continue to grow, innovate, and serve the people of the Midlands.
