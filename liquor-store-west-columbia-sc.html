Where is Express Liquor & Wine located in West Columbia, SC? +

We are located at 2201 Augusta Rd, West Columbia, SC 29169. We are easily accessible from West Columbia, Cayce, and downtown Columbia, with free parking available on-site. Get directions on Google Maps →

What are the store hours for Express Liquor & Wine? +

We are open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Sunday from 12:00 PM to 8:00 PM. We are open 7 days a week for your convenience, including most holidays.

What types of alcohol does Express Liquor & Wine carry? +

We carry a comprehensive selection including whiskey, bourbon, scotch, vodka, tequila, mezcal, rum, gin, cognac, brandy, wine (red, white, rosé, sparkling), domestic and imported beer, craft beer, cider, and liqueurs. We stock over 2,000 products from 50+ countries worldwide.

Does Express Liquor & Wine do special orders? +

Yes! If you're looking for a specific bottle or brand that we don't currently have in stock, give us a call at (803) 900-1358 and our team will do their best to source it or arrange a special order for you.

What is the legal drinking age in South Carolina? +

The legal drinking age in South Carolina is 21 years of age. We comply fully with all South Carolina ABC (Alcoholic Beverage Control) regulations and require valid government-issued ID for all alcohol purchases. We do not sell to anyone under the age of 21.

Is there parking available at Express Liquor & Wine? +

Yes! We have ample free parking available on-site at 2201 Augusta Rd. The store is also ADA accessible with a convenient entrance and wide aisles.
