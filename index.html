Our Story

West Columbia's Trusted Spirits Destination

Nestled on Augusta Road in the heart of West Columbia, Express Liquor & Wine has been serving the Midlands community with an extraordinary selection of beverages from around the globe.

  • Over 2,000 products spanning spirits, wines, beers & mixers
  • Sourced from more than 50 countries worldwide
  • Expert staff ready to help you find the perfect bottle
  • Competitive pricing with regular deals & promotions
  • Warm, welcoming atmosphere for every type of shopper
  • Proudly serving West Columbia & the greater Columbia metro
